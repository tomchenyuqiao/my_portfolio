Operating Lightships in Great Britain


Purpose


The goal of this project is to create a map accurately displaying a dataset of all operating lightships in Great Britain. The final product should demonstrate cartographic skills and presentation techniques of relevant data. (Introduction to Cartographic Design and Geovisualization)


Approach


Utilized ArcMap to visualize the lightship dataset, along with terrain data of the area. Made minor stylistic edits in Adobe Photoshop.
