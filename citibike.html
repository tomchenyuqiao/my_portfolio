Citi Bike and Weather Data Analysis


Purpose


This is a group project which our team (Team Dig Data) played a role as a consultant for Citi Bike. Our goal is to conduct a business analysis to study the impact of weather on Citi Bike ridership in Jersey City from January 2020 to June 2020.


Approach


Conducted exploratory data analysis and applied various machine learning algorithm in Jupyter Notebook on weather data and ridership counts.
