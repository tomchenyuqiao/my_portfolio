Air Pollution in China


Purpose


Particulate matter (PM2.5) refers to atmospheric particulate matter (PM) that have a diameter of less than 2.5 micrometers.
Measured by micrograms per cubic meter (ug/m^3), PM 2.5 is an air pollutant that is a concern for people's health and reduces visibility and cause the air to appear hazy when levels areelevated.

The goal of this project is to use weather data to model PM 2.5 concentration level in five cities from China, and determine the most successful meteorological indicator to PM 2.5 (Environmental Data Science)


Approach


Wrote a Python script in Jupyter Notebook to perform exploratory analysis and linear regression on the PM 2.5 dataset.
