Subway Accessibility for Persons of Limited Mobility


Purpose


The goal of this project is to create an exploratory dashboard to visualize New York City Motor Vehicle Collision statistics from 2012 to 2020. (Data Visualization)


Approach


Collected Motor Vehicle Collision data from New York City Open Data and created an interactive Tableau dashboard to visualize the dataset.


Final Product Overview


You may click on a specific zip code in the map to view associated injuries and fatalities. You may also click on a specific borough in the tree map to view associated injuries and fatalities.
