Subway Accessibility for Persons of Limited Mobility


Purpose


Collaborate with classmates to work on a selected topic. Transportation was our research topic with a focus on subway accessibility for persons of limited mobility. (Social Application of GIS)


Our research question were:

1. WHERE are persons of limited mobility located in relation to accessible subway entrances?

2. WHICH POPULATIONS have highest rates of inaccessibility?

3. WHICH COMMUNITY DISTRICTS have high percentages of persons of limited mobility who lack subway access?

Approach


We utilized ArcMap to perform buffer analysis, and true overlay to identify underserved community districts, and demographic compositions within.


Final Product Overview


Final poster was presented at the end of semester and displayed at Hunter College Geography Department.
