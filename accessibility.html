Accessibility Index for Accessible Subway Stations in Brooklyn to Population of Ambulatory Disability


Purpose


This project aims to analyze the accessibility of the twenty-two (22) accessible MTA subway stations to the population of ambulatory disability in Brooklyn. (Urban Application of GIS)


Approach


Obtained population of ambulatory disability data from the U.S. Census Bureau to calculate accessibility index of each accessible MTA subway stations in ArcMap.


Final Product Overview


Final map shown below displays all accessible stations in Brooklyn and their calculated accessibility index score with population of ambulatory disability displayed by census tract.
